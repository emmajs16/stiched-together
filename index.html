about

Women owned businesses are growing by a rate of 5%. We are here to support and foster a community of small business leaders, both locally and globally. stitched together strives to be a place where we can thread a strong community where you will feel encouraged and supported. We want to help you through the challenges and celebrations of starting a small business, or the next large corporation!

mission

Our mission is to create a positive impact on the next female entrepreneurs through connections, resources, and guides.

get started

To get started, create and account or log in. You will then have access to the many resources and discussions on stitched together. We encourage all of our users in the community to share your successess, struggles, and resources.
